Crusin' with Casey

November 6th, 2013, by Gabrielle Barandiaran

Our very own Casey Jabbour is co-oping with the Cunard cruise line. She has set sail on the Queen Victoria as the assistant printer for her last adventures as an official RIT student. Casey boarded the ship this past July and will be returning to American soil in January of 2014.

Thanks to our friend, the internet, and its cousin, social media, we are able to follow Casey along this once in a lifetime journey!

Follow Casey on her blog, Instagram and Twitter!
