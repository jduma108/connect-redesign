Connect to Present CaptuRIT at Imagine RIT 2014

April 11th, 2014, by Kathryn Cooper

With Imagine RIT only 25 days away it’s time to unveil this year’s project and give our readers a sneak peek at what wer are putting together. Our exhibit, entitled CaptuRIT, is a visual archive of digital ../assets that will capture the 2014 Imagine RIT Festival. Our goal with this year’s exhibit was to continue creating meaningful experiences that are fleeting in the moment but leave a lasting impact. We decided that our exhibit should aim to accomplish this objective for the whole festival!

CaptuRIT is a multi-faceted project that takes the Imagine RIT festival and allows visitors to relive the experience and see things they may have missed. Our fully automated photo booth experience gives visitors the opportunity to be photographed and become a part of the archive. Another important part of what we do each year is the takeaway, from your own font to a story book, we want to leave a lasting impression. The ../assets/img taken in the photo booth will be shared with visitors so they will have a way to remember their day spent at imagine. In order to fully capture all that Imagine RIT has to offer, photojournalists and other exhibitors will be documenting and submitting photos to be a part of the archive. The digital archive that will be created from all of these ../assets will encourage viewing, searching, and sharing after the event. Once the festival is over excerpts of the archive will be included in a physical time capsule that will be gifted to RIT in an effort to make this a truly long-lasting experience.

We hope to see all of you at our exhibit. Have your picture taken at our photo booth and become a part of RIT history!
