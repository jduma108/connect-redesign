About Us

What we are about…

Connect is a group of students from RIT’s College of Imaging Arts and Sciences who work to connect prospective and current students, staff/faculty, and alumni with what is going on in the School of Media Sciences. We hope from our site, you can preview organizations, guest speakers and presentations, and activities that go on here at RIT. The staff members are all involved with various organizations and groups, hoping to give you an inside look at the School of Media Sciences. If you have any questions or comments, email us at smsconnect@rit.edu.
