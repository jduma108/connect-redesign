Co-op

In the School of Print Media students are required to fulfill two semesters of cooperative education (co-op), which consists of ten weeks of employment within the industry. The co-op program provides students with first-hand experience in the workforce and allows students to apply what they’ve learned in the classroom to real life applications. For more information visit the RIT’s co-op website.
