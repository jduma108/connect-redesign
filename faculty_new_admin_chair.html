A Letter from the Administrative Chair

September 8th, 2015, by Megan Cooper

Welcome to the School of Media Sciences, SMS Connect Website.

In my capacity as Administrative Chair of the School my vision sees SMS as having two sets of customers, namely, our students, and, the companies in our industry who desire their skillsets and want to provide co-op and employment opportunities. Our goal is to provide the industry with the finest media solution providers available in print, web and mobile applications.

In my short time at RIT’s School of Media Sciences I realize how fortunate we are to have the high level of teaching and research provided by our School’s faculty members, and the impressive scholastic level of the students in our program.

It is my hope that you will come and visit us and discover the many opportunities available to you in your educational and professional career through RIT’s School of Media Sciences.

Sincerely,

  • Gregory S. D’Amico, Ph.D.
  • Administrative Chair
